## 1. Facility Profile

- **Type:** Community-Based Residential Facility (CBRF) — Assisted Living and Memory Care
- **Capacity:** 33 rooms; studio and one-bedroom units (22 floor plans listed on A Place for Mom)
- **Served populations:** Advanced aged, individuals with irreversible dementia/Alzheimer's, physically disabled, developmentally disabled, terminally ill residents; HCBS compliant for public funding
- **Care levels offered:**
  - **Assisted Living** — 24/7 caregiver support, medication management with nurse delegation oversight, health monitoring, fall prevention, incontinence care, ambulatory assistance
  - **Memory Care** — All AL services plus secured environment, delayed-egress doors, simplified wayfinding, visual cue systems; 5–6 structured programs per day, 7 days/week targeting cognitive engagement
  - **Respite Care** — Short-term stays available
- **Staffing:** 24/7 professional caregivers; nurse delegation model for medication management. No published RN on-site ratio; specific staffing numbers not publicly disclosed.
- **Payment:** HCBS compliant (Medicaid waiver eligible); check payments accepted; private pay confirmed. Medicare-certified nursing home status not claimed.
- **Technology:** Delayed-egress doors, visual wayfinding cues, and simplified room/common area layout designed specifically to reduce wandering and confusion for memory care residents.
- **Pricing:**

| Care Level | Starting Monthly Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Assisted Living | ~$7,000 | Studio; range varies by care needs |
| Memory Care | ~$10,000–$10,725 | Average across community ~$8,521 |

*FamilyAssets.com reports community cost range of $6,317–$10,725 with $8,521 average. A Place for Mom lists studio starting at $5,000; Caring.com lists AL starting at $7,000. Pricing appears to vary by room type and assessed care level.*

Dane County area average for assisted living is approximately $5,335–$5,665/month; Hattie's Glen runs above average, particularly for memory care.

## 6. Bottom Line

Hattie's Glen Waunakee is a purpose-built memory care and assisted living community in a quiet suburban Dane County setting, operated by NuCare Senior Living, which also runs well-reviewed facilities in the Madison area (notably Hatties Bay Middleton). The facility's HCBS compliance is a meaningful advantage for families who may need Medicaid waiver funding, and the structured daily programming and secured memory care environment suggest serious investment in cognitive care. The critical gap is the complete absence of third-party consumer reviews — there is simply no resident or family feedback from this Waunakee location against which to evaluate actual care quality, and chain-level employee reviews raise questions about management practices and compensation that may or may not apply here.

**Action item:** Before committing, request the full DQA survey history (license 0021211 via dhswebmaildqa@dhs.wisconsin.gov), ask specifically about registered nurse on-site availability and staff-to-resident ratios during evening and overnight shifts, and ask to speak with one or two current resident families — the facility's inability to point to any online reviews makes direct referrals especially important. Comparing with the Heritage Middleton CBRF, which has a visible inspection record and substantial consumer reviews, illustrates the additional research burden a no-review facility places on families.
